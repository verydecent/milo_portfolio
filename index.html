About

Milo Nichols graduated from California College of the Arts (CCA) with a BFA in Sculpture. His work is influenced by his upbringing in the San Fernando Valley. He spent his formative years exploring the hiking trails of the Santa Monica mountains, performing in high school theater, and doodling during class. It did not take long for him to realize his passion for fine arts. Once enrolled at CCA, Milo trained in a variety of disciplines including mold making, metal casting, welding, 3D printing, and electronics. After graduation, he returned to Los Angeles to show and produce new projects.
